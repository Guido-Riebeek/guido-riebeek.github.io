Plainly — Account Management

Request Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your Plainly account and all associated data at any time. Plainly is developed by Puredata OÜ, registered in Estonia, EU. We comply with GDPR and will process all deletion requests within 30 days.

How to request deletion

1
Send an email to info@puredata.ee with the subject line Account deletion request — Plainly.
2
Include the email address associated with your Plainly account so we can locate your data.
3
We will confirm receipt and process your request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

What gets deleted

Account credentials (email, password) Deleted within 30 days
Financial data (transactions, budgets, goals, accounts) Deleted within 30 days
Account preferences and settings Deleted within 30 days
Purchase and subscription records Retained by Google Play / RevenueCat per their policies

Purchase and subscription records are held by Google Play and RevenueCat and are outside our control. Deleting your Plainly account does not cancel an active subscription — you must cancel separately via Google Play. For questions, see our Privacy Policy.
